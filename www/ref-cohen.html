Cohen

Key Point
  • Examined four (4) different types of retraction devices for open surgery, Gyrus Protractor, Alexis Wound Retractor, Mobius Abdominal Retractor, Balfour metal retractor.
  • Total exposed surface area:
    - Gyrus Protractor: 93.9 cm.
    - Balfour: 79.3 cm.
    - Alexis: 77.0 cm.
    - Mobius: 61.0 cm.
  • The Gyrus Protractor provides improved exposure. "Its low profile design allows the surgeon to roll the ring tight to maximize the snugness against the incision, achieving the utmost exposure while minimizing hand fatigue and tissue damage during lengthy procedures."
  • The Gyrus Protractor maintains tissue moisture, which can decrease wound desiccation, and offers wound protection.
  • "The Gyrus Protractor has now become this surgeon's retractor of choice."
Reference
  • Cohen, S. & Schleifer W. Perfect Exposure For Open Surgery: A Prospective Trial Comparing Four Retractors. White Paper - 2007.
