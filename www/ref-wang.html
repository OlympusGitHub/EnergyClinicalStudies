Wang

Key Point
  • Compared the effectiveness of the PKS Cutting Forceps with the Kleppinger bipolar forceps in laparoscopically assisted vaginal hysterectomy (LAVH).
  • Average blood loss: PKS Cutting Forceps-197 mL; Kleppinger-253 mL (not statistically significant).
  • Average OR time: PKS-87.6 minutes; Kleppinger-93.4 minutes (not statistically significant).
  • PKS Cutting Forceps generated less plume and thermal tissue damage compared to the Kleppinger forceps.
  • PKS Cutting Forceps demonstrated less sticking and required fewer instrument changes than the Kleppinger forceps.
Reference
  • Wang C., Yuen L., Yen C., Lee C., Soong Y. Comparison of Efficacy of Pulsed Bipolar System and Conventional Electrosurgery in Laparoscopically Assisted Vaginal Hysterectomy. Laparoendoscopic & Advanced Surgical Techniques. 2005; 15 (4) p. 361-364.
