Ghomi

Key Point
  • Prospective study that examined the learning curve for laparoscopic supracervical hysterectomy (LSH).
  • 60 patients underwent LSH procedure.
  • The OR time was significantly reduced from 166 min. to 142.3 min. between the first 30 and last 30 cases.
  • Average uterine weight: First 30 cases-132.8 grams; last 30 cases-154.6 grams.
  • Average length of hospital stay: First 30 cases-23.6 hours; last 30 cases-23.3 hours.
  • Upper uterine pedicles are coagulated and transected by using a 5 mm PKS device (device not specified).
  • Harmonic Scalpel (Ethicon) used for amputation and Gynecare morcellator used for specimen removal (device not specified).
  • The authors concluded that 30 cases are needed for proficiency in laparoscopic supracervical hysterectomy and that OR time is reduced with experience.
  • For more information regarding LSH, please reference: Erian et al. (2008); Bojahr et al. (2009); Cipullo et al. (2009); Einarsson et al. (2010); Hoffman et al. (2005); Sarmini et al. (2005); and Zupi et al. (2003).
Reference
  • Ghomi A., Littman P., Prasad A., Einarsson J. Assessing The Learning Curve for Laparoscopic Supracervical Hysterectomy. J. of the Society of Laparoendoscopic Surgeons. 2007; 11; p. 190-194.
