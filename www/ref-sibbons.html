Sibbons

Key Point
  • Compares thermal margins between PKS Cutting Forceps, PKS Plasma Spatula and Harmonic ACE.
  • Thermal margins were comparable between all 3 devices.
    - PKS Cutting Forceps: 1.59 mm; PKS Plasma Spatula: 2.38 mm; Harmonic ACE 1.67 mm.
  • During coagulation and mechanical cutting, the PKS Cutting Forceps maximum device and tissue surface temperatures reached 75-83ºC, compared to 52-68ºC during cutting and 89-138ºC during coagulation for the Harmonic ACE.
  • "100ºC, represents a risk of internal thermal injury in the abdomiopelvic cavity."
  • Harmonic produced unintentional cavitational lesions, described as "cutting ahead of itself", which may be challenging for the surgeon to visualize.
  • Unintentional tissue cutting occurred with Harmonic when coagulation was applied, "...despite using the MIN setting".
  • Tissue may slip between the blades of the Harmonic ACE when cutting, leading to a decline in the precision of the instrument.
Reference
  • Sibbons P. Comparison of Hemostatic Sealing and Cutting Characteristics of Gyrus PK Devices with Harmonic Technologies. White Paper.
