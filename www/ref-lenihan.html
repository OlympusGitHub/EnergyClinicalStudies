Lenihan

Key Point
  • 113 patients, examined the learning curve when using the robot for LSH and TLH procedures.
  • 20 cases to set up robot in 45 min. or less with a dedicated robot team and 50 cases to improve to 35 min.
  • Mix of lap. procedures but a majority were LSH (31) and TLH (67).
  • Average Uterine Weight: 184 grams.
  • Conclusion states, "In the hands of surgeons with advanced laparoscopic skills, learning curve to stabilize operative times......is 50 cases."
  • For more information regarding robot learning curves please reference: Carreyrou (2010), Payne et al. (2008), and Steinberg et al. (2008).
Reference
  • Lenihan, J., Kovanda C., Seshadri-Kreaden, U. What is the Learning Curve for Robotic Assisted Gynecologic Surgery? J. Minimally Invasive Gynecology. 2008; 15 (5); p. 589-594.
